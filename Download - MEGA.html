    You are only eligible to participate in this referral program if:
    You have accepted our Terms of Service;
    Your MEGA account has not been suspended or terminated;
    You have made at least one payment by credit card or app-store to us for a MEGA plan (i.e., not in Bitcoin or any other crypto currency or by way of a voucher);
    You and the people you refer have complied and continue to comply with these referral program rules.
    For a referral to trigger a commission to you, that person must pay for a MEGA plan within 12 months of your referral.

    A qualifying commission will have a 90 day wait period before it becomes payable. Any breach of these rules within that period or otherwise means that the commission may be forfeited.

    Your accumulated commission balance must be at least 50 to qualify for a payment.

    All commission balances are gross balances. Where we are required by any law in any country to make a deduction or withholding from any payment due to you, we will do so and pay you the net amount.

    Depending on your country, we offer a variety of payment methods, including bank transfers and Bitcoin. For payments to some countries however we may also need to deduct MEGA, bank or other third party transaction or processing fees before paying you the net amount.

    You must comply with our Terms of Service to be eligible to receive any commission under this program.

    A legally valid takedown notice received by you means that you will forfeit any relating commissions you have earned.

    A legally valid takedown notice received by a person you have referred, or any other Terms of Service violation by that person, means that you will forfeit commissions you have earned from that person.

    A copyright strike on your account will disqualify your account from participation for 90 days, such that no new commissions can be earned even if referrals pay for a MEGA plan during this period.

    Repeated strikes or takedowns on your account will result in accumulated commissions being forfeited and we may suspend your account and disqualify you from participation in this referral program either temporarily or permanently at our discretion.

    Where we have paid commission to you for a referral and you or that person breaches our Terms of Service, we reserve the right to deduct that paid commission from future commissions payable to you.

    We reserve the right to advise people that you refer to MEGA under this referral program that you are receiving commission for doing so, as required by New Zealand law.

    We may close, suspend or change this program at any time. Changed rules will apply to all future referrals. If we permanently close this program we will pay out all commissions due for payment under these rules within 90 days of closure.

    Commission balances expire if not withdrawn within 2 years of being earned.

    These rules were adopted on 12 March 2020.
